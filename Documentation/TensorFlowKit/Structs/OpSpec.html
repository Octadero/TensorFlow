OpSpec is the specification of an Operation to be added to a Graph (using Graph.AddOperation).

  • input

    Inputs to this operation, which in turn must be outputs of other operations already added to the Graph.

    An operation may have multiple inputs with individual inputs being either a single tensor produced by another operation or a list of tensors produced by multiple operations. For example, the Concat operation takes two inputs: (1) the dimension along which to concatenate and (2) a list of tensors to concatenate.
